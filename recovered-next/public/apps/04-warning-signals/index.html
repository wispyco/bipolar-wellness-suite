Green Zone: Stable

Score 0-8

No significant warning signs. Continue your maintenance routine.

  • Continue regular sleep schedule
  • Take medications as prescribed
  • Maintain social rhythms and daily routines
  • Keep up with regular therapy appointments
  • Continue daily mood and warning sign monitoring

Yellow Zone: Early Warning

Score 9-18

Some warning signs are emerging. Time to increase self-care and vigilance.

  • Increase monitoring to twice daily (morning and evening)
  • Prioritize 8 hours of sleep -- strict bedtime routine
  • Reduce stimulation: limit caffeine, screen time, social commitments
  • Use CBT thought records if racing or negative thoughts appear
  • Contact your therapist or support person to discuss what you are noticing
  • Review and activate your personal coping strategies

Orange Zone: Escalating

Score 19-30

Multiple warning signs are active. Professional support is recommended.

  • Contact your psychiatrist or therapist today
  • Consider whether medication adjustment is needed
  • Ask a trusted person to check in with you daily
  • Postpone major decisions and new commitments
  • Remove access to credit cards if mania signs are prominent
  • Increase structure: write out a simple daily plan
  • Avoid alcohol and recreational substances

Red Zone: Crisis Level

Score 31+

Severe warning signs indicate a possible episode. Immediate professional intervention is needed.

  • Contact your psychiatrist or crisis team immediately
  • Call 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline) if you are in danger
  • Go to the emergency room if needed
  • Activate your safety plan (see Crisis Planner app)
  • Ask someone to stay with you
  • Do not make any major life decisions
  • Ensure medications are being taken and not hoarded

This tool is for self-monitoring and education. It is not a diagnostic tool. If you are in crisis, call 988 or go to your nearest emergency room.
