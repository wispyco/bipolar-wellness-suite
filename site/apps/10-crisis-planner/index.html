Crisis & Support Planner

Based on the Stanley & Brown Safety Planning Intervention. Having a written plan ready before a crisis occurs is one of the most effective suicide prevention strategies.

The Stanley & Brown Safety Plan has 6 steps, used in order during a crisis. Complete each step now, while you are stable, so the plan is ready when you need it.
